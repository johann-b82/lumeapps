# Introduction

Welcome to the KPI Dashboard user guide. This app lets your team upload sales data files, explore revenue KPIs on an interactive dashboard, and review HR metrics sourced directly from Personio — all in one place. Whether you are uploading your first data file or customising chart views, this guide walks you through every feature step by step.

## What You'll Find

- **[Uploading Data](/docs/user-guide/uploading-data)** — Learn how to drag and drop or browse for a `.csv` or `.txt` file, understand upload states and error messages, and manage your upload history.

- **[Sales Dashboard](/docs/user-guide/sales-dashboard)** — Explore the three revenue KPI cards, the "Revenue over time" chart, date range presets, and the searchable orders table.

- **[HR Dashboard](/docs/user-guide/hr-dashboard)** — Understand the five HR KPI cards powered by Personio, how to trigger a data refresh, and how to read the employee table.

- **[Filters & Date Ranges](/docs/user-guide/filters)** — A full walkthrough of date presets, custom date ranges, and chart type toggles (Bar / Area).

- **[Language & Dark Mode](/docs/user-guide/language-and-theme)** — Switch the interface between English and German, and toggle between light and dark mode.

## Tips for Getting Started

> **Tip:** Start with [Uploading Data](/docs/user-guide/uploading-data) if you have not yet added any sales records. The dashboards display "No data yet" until at least one file is imported.

> **Note:** The Upload page is only available to Admin users. If you see a permissions message, ask your administrator to upload the data file on your behalf.
